About Me

I'm Jordan and I'm a Creative

Born and raised in Charlotte NC, I have always had a passion for creation. Starting with singing in the church at a young age and moving into musical theater in high school and college, arts and creation have always been my driving force. During the COVID-19 pandemic I found a tying thread to my passion for creativity, technology. I entered into a technology apprenticeship with Red Ventures, which taught me various coding languages and professional development skills that I then leveraged into a associate software engineer position with Lowe's. My time there taught me very valuable skills as well as necessary working experience with new technology frameworks, languages and other tools such as Jira, Docker, MaterialUI, Figma, MySql, Jenkins and more. I am currently a student in the AWS Re/Start program with the goal of earning my AWS Cloud Practioner Certification. When I am not working on projects I am reviewing various movies, music and TV shows on my podcast. I enjoy traveling with friends, cooking, and a good laugh.

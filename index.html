About Me

I'm Jordan and I'm a Creative

Born and raised in Charlotte NC, I have always had a passion for creation. Starting with singing in the church at a young age and moving into musical theater in high school and college, arts and creation have always been my driving force. During the COVID-19 pandemic I found a tying thread to my passion for creativity, technology. I entered into the Road to Hire apprenticeship, which taught me various coding languages and professional development skills that I have now leveraged into a desire for working in the technology field. When I am not working on projects I am reviewing various movies, music and TV shows on my podcast. I enjoy traveling with friends and being spontaneous to the things that this world has to offer.

My certifications

Agile Project Management

Agile methodology is a type of project management process where demands and solutions evolve through the collaborative effort of self-organizing and cross-functional teams and their customers.

Growth Driven Design

Growth Driven Design is agile development or redesign of a website in intentional increments. Making continuous, adaptations based on data, this is not laboring to make every element of your site perfect before leaving it to sit static online for a few years.
